Predict Autism Risk

Our autism prediction tool is designed to help identify individuals who may be at a high risk of autism. By leveraging data collected from various tests and evaluations, our machine learning model analyzes multiple factors to provide a risk assessment. Early detection of autism is crucial as it allows for timely interventions and support, which can significantly improve the quality of life for individuals with autism and their families. The prediction process involves inputting the patient's unique ID, which is then used to retrieve and process their data through our sophisticated algorithms. The results indicate whether the patient is at a high or low risk of autism, enabling healthcare providers and caregivers to make informed decisions about further evaluations and interventions.
