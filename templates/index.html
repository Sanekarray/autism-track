About

Autism Track

Autism Track is an easy-to-use app that helps find early signs of autism with smart data tools. By spotting these signs early, we can provide quick support and personalized care to improve the lives of people with autism. Our goal is to help everyone affected by autism live a better and more independent life.

Early Autism Detection

Using Data for Accurate Predictions

Thanks to multiple data sources, healthcare professionals can access a comprehensive dashboard and predictive tools. By entering factors such as ASD tests, jaundice, and family relationships, the system can predict the likelihood of autism.

Best Laboratory

Tests Available

Our advanced autism detection system evaluates a range of scores from A1 to A10 to provide a comprehensive assessment of autism. These scores help in understanding different aspects of autism and guide personalized care plans for better outcomes. Each score reflects a specific area of behavior and development, offering a detailed overview to support accurate diagnosis and intervention.
